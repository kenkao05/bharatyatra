Travel & Stay Tips

Best Time to Visit

October – March (cool weather, ideal for sightseeing). Avoid summer (April-June, very hot). Special festivals like Diwali in Ayodhya, Dev Deepawali in Varanasi.

Booking Tips

Book hotels in Agra, Varanasi, and Lucknow in advance during peak tourist season. Taj Mahal tickets can be booked online to save time.

Local Food

Lucknowi Biryani, Kebabs (Tunday, Galawati), Chaat, Petha (Agra sweet), Banarasi Paan, Kachori-Sabzi. Street food culture is vibrant.

Transport

Major airports in Lucknow, Varanasi, and Agra. Excellent train connectivity across the state. Metro in Lucknow and Noida. Taxis and app-cabs widely available.
