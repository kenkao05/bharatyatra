Travel & Stay Tips

Best Time to Visit

October – March (pleasant weather, festivals like Navratri, Rann Utsav). Avoid summer (very hot).

Booking Tips

For Rann of Kutch and Gir National Park, book safaris/hotels in advance, especially during peak season.

Local Food

Gujarati thali, Dhokla, Khandvi, Fafda, Undhiyu, and sweets like Mohanthal. Cities have great restaurants; local dhabas offer authentic taste.

Transport

Ahmedabad is the main entry (airport, rail hub). Well-connected roads to Dwarka, Somnath, Gir, and Kutch. Rental cars recommended for flexibility.
