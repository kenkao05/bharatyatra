Travel & Stay Tips

Best Time to Visit

March – October (pleasant weather, blooming gardens). December – February for snow and skiing. Avoid monsoon (July-August) due to landslides.

Booking Tips

For Gulmarg, Pahalgam, and Dal Lake houseboats, book in advance during peak season. Check travel advisories before planning.

Local Food

Kashmiri Wazwan (multi-course meal), Rogan Josh, Yakhni, Kahwa tea, Modur Pulav. Srinagar has great restaurants and street food.

Transport

Srinagar is the main entry (airport). Well-connected roads to Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonamarg. Hire taxis or use local transport. Permit required for some areas.
