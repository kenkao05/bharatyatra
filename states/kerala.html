Travel & Stay Tips

Best Time to Visit

September – March (post-monsoon, pleasant weather). June – August for monsoon beauty and Ayurveda treatments. Avoid peak summer (April-May).

Booking Tips

Book houseboats in Alleppey/Kumarakom in advance. For Munnar and Wayanad hill stations, pre-book during peak season and festivals.

Local Food

Kerala Sadya (banana leaf feast), Appam with stew, Puttu, Fish Moilee, Karimeen Pollichathu. Try coastal seafood and traditional toddy shops.

Transport

Kochi and Trivandrum are main airports. Well-connected by trains and buses. Rent cars or use taxis. Backwater ferries offer unique travel experience.
