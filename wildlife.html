Jim Corbett National Park

Uttarakhand - Nainital & Pauri Garhwal Region

Park Information

Jim Corbett National Park

Location: Nainital / Pauri Garhwal region; nearest town Ramnagar.

Key Species: Bengal tiger, elephant, leopard, deer species; >500 bird species.

Area: ~1,318 km² (Corbett Tiger Reserve complex).

Best Time: Oct–June (peak wildlife sightings in winter / dry months).

How to Reach: Nearest rail/road hub Ramnagar; nearest airport Pantnagar (≈70–100 km) or Delhi (≈260 km).

Kaziranga National Park

Assam - Golaghat & Nagaon Districts

Park Information

Kaziranga National Park

Location: Golaghat & Nagaon districts, Assam.

Key Species: Indian one-horned rhinoceros (iconic), tigers, elephants, vast birdlife.

Area: ~430 km² (core + larger tiger reserve area).

Best Time: Nov–April (park closed during monsoon season for safety).

How to Reach: Nearest city Golaghat / Jorhat; Jorhat & Guwahati airports serve visitors.

Ranthambore National Park

Rajasthan - Sawai Madhopur District

Park Information

Ranthambore National Park

Location: Sawai Madhopur, Rajasthan (Ranthambore Fort inside park).

Key Species: Bengal tiger (excellent sightings), sambar, spotted deer, various birds.

Area: Tiger reserve & national park complex (~1,334 km² including buffer).

Best Time: Oct–June (hot in summer; best tiger sightings Nov–Mar).

How to Reach: Nearest town Sawai Madhopur (railway); Jaipur / Delhi airports by road/rail.

Bandhavgarh National Park

Madhya Pradesh - Umaria District

Park Information

Bandhavgarh National Park

Location: Umaria district / Madhya Pradesh; nearest town Tala & Bandhavgarh Fort area.

Key Species: High density of tigers, leopard, deer species; historic fort inside park.

Area: Tiger reserve with core & buffer; popular for tiger safaris.

Best Time: Oct–Jun (closed/limited during rains).

How to Reach: Nearest rail Urwan / Umaria; Jabalpur or Khajuraho airports.

Kanha National Park

Madhya Pradesh - Mandla & Balaghat Districts

Park Information

Kanha National Park

Location: Mandla & Balaghat districts (central India).

Key Species: Bengal tiger, barasingha (hardground swamp deer — conservation success), leopard, sloth bear.

Area: Large central India reserve—one of India's model parks (basis for Kipling's "Mowgli" landscapes).

Best Time: Oct–Jun.

How to Reach: Nearest rail station Mandla or Jabalpur (rail & airport).

Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve

Maharashtra - Chandrapur District

Park Information

Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve

Location: Chandrapur district, Maharashtra.

Key Species: Bengal tiger (one of Maharashtra's best tiger spotting reserves), gaur, sloth bear, many birds.

Area: Large tiger reserve made of Tadoba NP + Andhari sanctuary.

Best Time: Oct–June (safari schedules vary; check local booking).

How to Reach: Nearest city Chandrapur; Nagpur airport ~140–160 km.

Pench National Park

Madhya Pradesh - MP/Maharashtra Belt

Park Information

Pench National Park

Location: Straddles MP / Maharashtra areas (Pench Tiger Reserve).

Key Species: Tigers, leopard, sloth bear, deer, rich birdlife — inspiration for Kipling's "The Jungle Book".

Area: Important central India tiger reserve corridor.

Best Time: Oct–Jun.

How to Reach: Nearest rail/road hubs Nagpur / Seoni / Jabalpur depending on approach.

Gir National Park

Gujarat - Junagadh & Gir Somnath

Park Information

Gir National Park

Location: Junagadh / Gir Somnath districts, Gujarat (Sasan Gir area).

Key Species: Asiatic lion (unique global population), leopard, deer, nilgai.

Area: Protected national park + large wildlife sanctuary area.

Best Time: Oct–June (avoid monsoon).

How to Reach: Nearest towns: Junagadh, Veraval; Rajkot / Diu airports for access.

Sundarbans National Park

West Bengal - South 24 Parganas

Park Information

Sundarbans National Park

Location: Sundarbans delta, South 24 Parganas (Indian Sundarbans).

Key Species: Bengal tiger (mangrove/tidal tigers), saltwater crocodile, many aquatic birds, unique mangrove flora.

Area: Mangrove delta, biosphere reserve—shared ecosystem with Bangladesh.

Best Time: Nov–Mar (many seasonal closures during monsoon).

How to Reach: Nearest hub Kolkata → boat/launch to Sundarban entry points (Hogla, Godkhali, Sajnekhali etc.).

Periyar Tiger Reserve

Kerala - Idukki & Pathanamthitta

Park Information

Periyar Tiger Reserve

Location: Idukki & Pathanamthitta districts, Kerala (Western Ghats).

Key Species: Elephants, Bengal tiger, gaur, rich endemic bird & plant life. Famous for boat safaris on Periyar Lake.

Area: ~350 km² protected area (part of Western Ghats biodiversity hotspot).

Best Time: Sep–May (monsoon heavy June–Aug but waterfall scenery is great then).

How to Reach: Nearest town Kumily / Thekkady; nearest airports Kochi / Madurai.

Bandipur National Park

Karnataka - Mysore & Chamarajanagar

Park Information

Bandipur National Park

Location: Mysore / Chamarajanagar region, Karnataka; contiguous with Nagarhole and Mudumalai (TN).

Key Species: Tigers, elephants, gaurs, deer, rich birdlife — part of Nilgiri-Biosphere corridors.

Area: Major protected area in southern India (core of the Mysore forest belt).

Best Time: Oct–May (monsoon months limit access).

How to Reach: Nearest city Mysore (≈80–100 km), Bangalore airport farther away.

Nagarhole National Park

Karnataka - Kodagu & Mysore Districts

Park Information

Nagarhole National Park

Location: Kodagu & Mysore districts, Karnataka (Kabini reservoir area).

Key Species: Bengal tiger, Asian elephant, leopard, gaur, rich riverine birdlife; excellent wildlife corridor with Bandipur.

Area: Part of Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve; famous for boat safaris on Kabini and jeep safaris.

Best Time: Oct–May.

How to Reach: Nearest town Hunsur / Mysore; access via Kabini & Nagarhole entry points.
