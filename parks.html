Important Information for Visitors

Before You Visit

  • Check park opening dates (many close during monsoon)
  • Book safaris well in advance for popular parks
  • Verify permit requirements for certain zones
  • Check weather conditions before traveling

Safety Guidelines

  • Follow guide instructions strictly
  • Never exit vehicle during safari
  • Maintain silence to avoid disturbing wildlife
  • Keep safe distance from all animals

What to Bring

  • Binoculars for better wildlife viewing
  • Camera with telephoto lens
  • Neutral-colored clothing
  • Sun protection & insect repellent

Prohibited Items

  • Plastic items & single-use plastics
  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Tobacco products
  • Loud music systems
